About Pastor Ashish Mittal

Pastor & Chairman of Ashish Mittal Foundation

Pastor Ashish Mittal, son of Pastor Kanchan Mittal was born on 11 May 2000 in Lehragaga, a town in the state of Punjab, India. He witnessed his mother’s sacrifice for the kingdom of God from the very beginning of his life. From a very young age, Pastor Ashish Mittal experienced trials with Pastor Kanchan Mittal as she was set on the way of God. Walking long distances with patience and living in coarse environments, he learned endurance and the importance of standing steadfast in the word of God.

By the time Pastor Ashish turned 7 years old, he was admitted to one of the best schools of India, the Sherwood College, Nainital. He was taught good character with his mother’s stick and the word of God. He tells, “Mom pushed us to move towards perfection. She would rebuke us with a stick whenever necessary to put us back on track”. Even in a lucrative environment, Pastor Ashish Mittal remained in the periphery of the Word of God to lead an exemplary student life amidst temptation. He received laurels for performing exceptionally in sports especially hockey, football, basketball and athletics.

Pastor Ashish Mittal shares a story of his communication with his mother during his school days. “One thing that mom would always ask (when she called us on phone) was whether or not we read bible or attended chapel everyday”. He truly analyzed the spiritual lifestyle of his mother who could give up anything for God. Pastor Kanchan Mittal proudly shares the story of sacrifice of her elder sons, “God wanted the children to help out in the ministry. I called them on the phones and told them that God wants them out of the college and in the works of church. They said that if this is the will of God, we will do it”. Pastor Ashish Mittal left Sherwood College and completed his education from a school near the ministry in Punjab, India. He further graduated with a degree in bachelors of commerce from Chandigarh in the year 2019 followed by M.com. from Panjab University, Chandigarh. He’s now pursuing his Ph.D. from Chitkara University. All of this while working for the kingdom of God.

Pastor Ashish Mittal has been a pillar of the ministry since his exit from Sherwood College. He performs all legal and management duties of the ministry, along with being a worshiper, preacher, motivator and media manager. He has been a guide for youth, encouraging them to lead disciplined and successful life. He has been empowering the youth, orphans, and children of low income group, widows, aged and other weaker sections of the society. In the year 2020, Pastor Ashish Mittal founded the Ashish Mittal Foundation. The foundation has been striving to provide all amenities including groceries, clothing, skills and education, indiscriminately to all in need. Many testify to his endurance, patience, kindness, obedience and faithfulness; and he is relentlessly moving forward in pursuit of perfection.

Join in Social Welfare Causes

Ashish Mittal Foundation is a not-for-profit organisation founded in the year 2020 by the youth of Punjab. We aim to eliminate hunger and be catalysts of change for the society by making the socio-economically weaker sections financially independent through our skilling programs. We also facilitate health, drug and environment awareness campaigns. AMF has collaborated with villages and is set to establish community libraries to encourage education and literacy among rural children, youth and women.
